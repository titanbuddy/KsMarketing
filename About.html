ABOUT US

OUR STORY

Established in 2007

Starting a real estate business seemingly was a remote possibility for Mr. Usman Alam, founder of KS Marketing. However, the determined alumni of Quaid e Azam University made his mark eventually in the tumultuous and competitive world of Pakistan’s real estate in 2004.

Expanded in 2015

18 years down the road KS Marketing now has an impressive profile of launching more than 50 housing society projects which easily dwarfs many leading marketing brands. To meet the increasing demand for real estate development in the country, KS Marketing became a real estate developer in 2020.

developer transition in 2021

A new era began with four promised projects delivered in the University Town Islamabad, which are now giving possession to owners as well.

Full spectrum Developer

In 2023, KS Marketing successfully launched KS View in Parkview City’s Commercial Downtown. A marvelous piece of construction offering residential and commercial units. However, KS Hills remains its game-changer project in 2024, which will deliver 17 state-of-the-art high-rise towers for residential, corporate, and commercial outlets for many international brands. The future goals of KS Marketing include six more projects in the capital city, Islamabad, while the company is also forging international partnerships to expand its investment portfolio.
