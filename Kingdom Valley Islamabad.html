Kingdom Valley Islamabad

Introduction

Kingdom Valley Islamabad is a PHATA-approved, state-of-the-art housing society located conveniently close to the M-2 Lahore-Islamabad Motorway. Developed by Kingdom Group, this flagship project combines modern design with affordable luxury and is built in collaboration with the Naya Pakistan Housing Scheme. The developers aim to deliver high-quality services at accessible prices.

    Kingdom Valley Islamabad NOC

    The No Objection Certificate (NOC) for Kingdom Valley Islamabad is reportedly approved under the Naya Pakistan Housing Scheme, with registration number "DRG/PHATA/2176-2021." It is also approved by the Punjab Housing and Town Planning Agency (PHATA). According to the Punjab Government's official website, the NOC status for Kingdom Valley Phase II and Phase III is currently under process, ensuring rapid development of the project.
