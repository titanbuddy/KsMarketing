Lahore Smart City

Introduction

Lahore Smart City (LSC) is an LDA-approved housing society located at Lahore Bypass, jointly developed by FDH and HRL. It is the second smart city in Pakistan and the only one in Lahore. The LSC has brought an alternative lifestyle to the real estate industry with all the modern facilities and amenities.

Lahore Smart City guarantees high returns on investment (ROI) and an incredibly comfortable lifestyle. It offers cutting-edge services, seamless connectivity, and high-quality infrastructure. The flagship project of FDHL and HRL group, LSC, offers potential buyers or investors the most reliable long-term investment opportunities.

About Lahore Smart City Developers & Owners

World-class Future Development Holdings PVT Ltd. (FDHL), Surbana Jurong (SJ), and Habib Rafique Pvt developed the Lahore Smart City. Limited (HRL). They are already well-known in the real estate industry because of their hard work and commitment to the high-quality development of infrastructure in residential societies such as DHA and Bahria Town.

The developers have previously constructed several successful world-class property projects in Pakistan and abroad. They also pledge to uphold the communities’ living standards at the highest global level.

    Lahore Smart City Booking Procedure

    The booking procedure for LSC plots is straightforward. Follow these steps:

    1. Fill out your booking application form with full attention
    2. Attach the CNIC copies of the applicant
    3. Pay down payment via cheque or pay order in favor of “Lahore Smart City” (confirm with management for any updates)
    4. After confirmation, cash payments are also accepted
    5. Submit all required documents and payment, and receive the receipt

    Documentation Requirement for Booking

    The following documents are required to buy property in Lahore Smart City:

    • Two passport-size pictures
    • NICOP for overseas clients
    • Two copies of your National Identity Card
    • Two copies of the I.D card of your next kin

    Frequently Asked Questions (FAQs)

    Following are the FAQs about Capital Hills Islamabad:

    Q1: Who are the developers of Lahore Smart City?

    Ans. Future Development Holdings (FDH) and Habib Rafiq Limited (HRL), renowned names in Pakistan’s real estate industry, are developing Lahore Smart City.

    Q2: Is Lahore Smart City approved by the Lahore Development Authority (LDA)?

    Ans. Yes, Lahore Smart City has received its No Objection Certificate (NOC) from the Lahore Development Authority (LDA).

    Q3: Where is Lahore Smart City located?

    Ans. LSC is located on the Lahore Bypass near Kala Shah Kaku, offering easy access to the M2 Motorway and Lahore’s significant suburbs.

    Q4: What plot sizes are available in Lahore Smart City?

    Ans. The project currently offers residential plots of 5 Marla, 7 Marla, 10 Marla, 12 Marla, and 1 Kanal.

    Q5: How can I book a plot in Lahore Smart City?

    Ans. To book a plot, you can contact authorized sales partners or the management office of Lahore Smart City. The booking starts with a 10% down payment.

    Q6: What is the installment plan for Lahore Smart City?

    Ans. The project offers a flexible 3-year installment plan for residential and commercial plots with quarterly payments.

    Q7: How much land have the authorities designated for Lahore Smart City?

    Ans. Lahore Smart City spans 20,000 Kanals of land, with plans for expansion.

    Q8: What are the development charges for Lahore Smart City?

    Ans. Development charges vary based on plot size and location within the society. They cover infrastructure like roads, electricity, water supply, and sewerage.

    Q9: What is the e-tracking facility in Lahore Smart City?

    Ans. The e-tracking facility allows investors and plot holders to track the status of their plots and payments online via the official website.

    Q10: How can I pay for my plot in Lahore Smart City?

    Ans. Payments can be made via bank transfer, remittance, or pay order in favor of Future Development Holdings (FDH).

    Q11: What amenities does Lahore Smart City offer?

    Ans. Lahore Smart City will offer state-of-the-art amenities such as smart security, a central park, healthcare facilities, educational institutions, and entertainment areas.

    Q12: How credible are the developers of Lahore Smart City?

    Ans. The developers, HRL, have a credible reputation and experience in delivering major projects like DHA, Bahria Town, and motorway development.

    Q13: Is Lahore Smart City a good investment?
